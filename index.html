Introduction

Tony is an invigorating artist based in Gloucestershire. His work celebrates the pleasure of movement and colour through an original and vibrant style. He creates work which is ambitious, unrestrained, determined and exciting.

Works on the carousel opposite are detailed in Paintings-Life and Drawings-Life .

Recent exhibitions

Tony's figurative paintings were exhibited at PARAGON Gallery, Montpellier, Cheltenham as guest artist in the gallery's "Cotswold Artists" exhibition in May 2017. The works were very well received, in particular the sale of the largest painting "Maybe" which filled the window.

Tony's figurative paintings were exhibited  in the Affordable Art Fair  in Battersea Park, London in  October 2017.
