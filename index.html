Tony is an invigorating artist based in Gloucestershire. His work celebrates the pleasure of movement and colour through an original and vibrant style. He creates work which is ambitious, unrestrained, determined and exciting.

Recent exhibition

Tony's figurative paintings were exhibited at PARAGON Gallery, Montpellier, Cheltenham as guest artist in the gallery's "Cotswold Artists" exhibition from Friday 21 April to Saturday 6 May 2017. The works were very well received, in particular the sale of the largest painting "Maybe" which filled the window.

Forthcoming exhibition

Tony's figurative paintings and drawings will be exhibited  in the Affordable Art Fair  in Battersea Park, London in the Paragon Gallery's stand number I5 (letter I number 5) from Wednesday 18 to Sunday 22 October 2017.
